Welcome, User
▶ Overview
M.A.I.G is a digital archive designed to provide detailed insight into modern and historical military weapon systems, enhancing research and knowledge about global defense technologies.
▶ Purpose
The goal of this platform is to compile, visualize, and organize data about weapons, vehicles, and defense systems for academic and analytical use.
▶ Weapon Categories
1. Small Arms (Pistols, Rifles, SMGs)
2. Artillery Systems
3. Armored Vehicles
4. Naval Equipment
5. Airborne Weapons
6. Missile and Rocket Systems
▶ Technology Stack
• HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for front-end
• Future upgrade: React.js + Node.js
• Optional backend expansion using MongoDB for weapon data indexing.
▶ Future Upgrades
• 3D visualization of weapons
• Real-time comparison tool
• Secure user login for researchers
• Integrated defense news feed and analytics.
